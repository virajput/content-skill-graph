# Builders

## Who They Are
- indie hackers
- AI engineers
- SaaS founders
- operators
- freelancers monetizing tech skills

## What They Want
- actionable playbooks
- real numbers
- tools they can use today
- systems that save time
- workflows that scale without headcount

## What They Hate
- vague motivation
- abstract strategy without implementation
- fake gurus
- theory with no proof

## Messaging Notes
Talk directly. Show proof. Lead with utility. Make them feel like they can apply the idea today.
