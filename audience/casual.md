# Casual Audience

## Who They Are
- curious about AI, technology and system architecture
- creators exploring better workflows
- beginners who want a simpler entry point
- people inspired by automation but not building full products yet

## What They Want
- simplified explanations
- aspirational examples
- clear starting points
- "wow I can do this too" moments

## What They Hate
- jargon-heavy explanations
- content that assumes too much prior knowledge
- long technical tangents without payoff

## Messaging Notes
Use simpler language, more context, and more inspiration. Reduce complexity without losing the core lesson.
